  • Lechon Kawali

    Popular Filipino pork dish called lechon kawali is deep-fried to a golden-brown perfection.
    It makes a wonderful appetizer or main dish since it is golden and crunchy on the outside and
    juicy and flavorful on the inside.

    Ingredients

    • Pork Belly
    • Salt
    • Whole Peppercorn
    • Water
    • Cooking Oil
    How to Cook:
    1. Boil Pork Belly with Peppercorn, Bay Leaves, and Salt until Tender
    2. Strain Pork Belly and let it cool down
    3. Heat oil in cooking pan
    4. Put the pork belly in the cooking pan
    5. Strain the pork
    6. Optional : Double fry to make it crispier
    7. Serve and Enjoy!
