  • Pork Menudo

    a Filipino pork stew dish with carrots and potato. a deliciously rich stew made
    with liver and pig on a tomato base. Considering the simplicity of the ingredients
    used, this traditional Filipino menudo meal will surprise you with its incredible flavors!

    Ingredients

    • Pork
    • Fish Sauce
    • Pepper
    • Tomato paste
    • Tomatoes
    • Garlic
    • Yellow onions
    • Potatoes (Cubed)
    • Bell pepper
    • Green peas
    • Carrots
    How to Cook:
    1. Heat the cooking oil in a cooking pan
    2. Saut√© the onion, garlic, tomatoes, and tomato paste until paste like texture.
    3. Put-in the pork and add water and boil until tender.
    4. After the pork becomes tender, Add the potatoes and carrots until they are eatable soft.
    5. Optional: Add Raisins or Liver.
    6. After the potatoes and carrots are soft. Adjust the taste according to your preference using pepper and fish sauce.
    7. Serve and Enjoy!
