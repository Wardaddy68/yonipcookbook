    • Ginisang Upo

    Ginisang Upo is bottle gourd sautéed in garlic, onions, and tomatoes.
    This Ginisang Upo recipe also makes use of ground pork for additional
    flavor and texture.

    Ingredients

    • Upo (white squash)
    • Garlic
    • Tomatoes
    • Onions
    • Ground Pork
    • Salt
    • Cooking oil
    • Pepper
    How to Cook:
    1. heat oil in a cooking pan.
    2. Sauté the onions, garlics, and tomatoes.
    3. Add the ground pork and put some black pepper and sauté until golden brown.
    4. Add water then the upo. Cook it according to your preference of texture.
    5. Adjust the taste according to your preference with salt and pepper.
    6. Eat and Enjoy!
