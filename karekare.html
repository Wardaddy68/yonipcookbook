  • Kare - Kare

    The Kare-Kare is curry-like stew made from ground peanuts
    and it has different meats and vegetables and being served
    with thick and savory sauce from the food itself.

    Ingredients

    • Onion
    • Garlic
    • Salt
    • Oil
    • Water
    • Peanuts
    • Beef
    • Tripe
    • Peanut Butter
    • Cooked Bagoong Alamang
    • Atsuete
    • Pechay
    • String Beans
    • Okra
    How to Cook:
    1. Boil the Beef and Tripe or Pork in water for an hour or until cooked.
    2. Strain and keep the stock.
    3. Heat oil and atsuete seeds in a pan. Removed the seeds.
    4. Saut√© garlic, onions until golden brown.
    5. Add the stock and peanut butter. Add the beef and tripe or Pork.
    6. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minutes.
    7. Salt to taste.
    8. Add the Eggplant, String Beans, Bok Choy and Okra (If available only).
    9. Cook the vegetables for 5 minutes. Do not overcook the vegetables.
    10. Optional : Adjust the consistency using Cornstarch and Water mixture
    11. Serve with Rice and Bagoong.
