  • Dinakdakan

    This is a deviation from the authentic recipe in which pork mask, liver,
    and innards are commonly utilized. This dish has the same delicious
    taste and it should be easier to prepare.

    Ingredients

    • Liempo
    • Mayonnaise
    • Knorr Seasoning
    • Calamansi
    • Red chili peppers and Long green chili peppers
    • Onions or Yellow onions
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    How to Cook:
    1. Boil pork belly until tender with salt.
    2. Grill the pork belly. After grilling, cut it into bite size.
    3. Make a mixture of Mayonnaise, Seasoning, pepper, calamansi, and add whatever you want and mix it well.
    4. Cut the chilies and onions and mix all the ingredients and adjust the taste according to your preference.
    5. Eat and Enjoy!
