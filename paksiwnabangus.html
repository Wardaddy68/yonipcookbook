    • Paksiw na Bangus

    Bangus Paksiw is a Filipino fish stew made of milkfish, vinegar, and spices.
    It's a hearty, tasty, and budget-friendly meal that's perfect with steamed rice.

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 lbs. milkfish
    • 3 thumbs crushed ginger
    • 5 cloves garlic crushed
    • 1/2 cup white vinegar
    • 1 cup water
    • 1 piece onion sliced
    • 1 piece eggplant
    • 5 pieces okra
    • 2 pieces long green pepper
    • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn
    How to Cook:
    1. Arrange ginger, garlic, okra, onion, long green pepper, peppercorn, and eggplant on the pot.
    2. Top with bangus slices.
    3. Pour water and vinegar. Apply heat and let boil. Cover and continue to cook
      in low to medium heat for 15 minutes.
    4. Add salt to taste.
    5. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve.
